Our Story

From a simple idea to a leading tech brand

2025

The Beginning

Voxio was founded with a simple mission: to create tech products that seamlessly integrate into modern lifestyles. Our first product was a pair of wireless earbuds that combined premium sound with practical design.

2025

First Major Success

The launch of our Overdrive Headphones marked our entry into the premium audio market. With industry-leading noise cancellation and 40-hour battery life, they quickly became a customer favorite.

2025

Expanding Horizons

We introduced the Chrono Smartwatch, combining health tracking with smart features. This marked our expansion beyond audio into wearable technology, receiving critical acclaim for its design and functionality.

2026

Today & Beyond

With over 1 million products sold worldwide, Voxio continues to innovate. We're currently developing next-generation smart home devices and exploring AI integration in our product ecosystem.
