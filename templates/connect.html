Where can I buy Voxio products?

Voxio products are available on major online retailers including Amazon, Walmart, Best Buy, and eBay. You can also purchase directly from authorized resellers in select countries.

What is your warranty policy?

All Voxio products come with a 2-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. The warranty does not cover accidental damage, water damage (beyond specified ratings), or normal wear and tear.

How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary by location and retailer. Most orders within the US arrive within 3-5 business days. International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days depending on the destination.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide through our retail partners. Shipping costs and delivery times vary by country. Please check with the specific retailer for international shipping details.
